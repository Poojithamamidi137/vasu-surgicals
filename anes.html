ANESTHESIA MACHINE

UHCP MARK V

UHCP Mark V Anesthesia Machine is a robust and economical solution designed for operation theatres and critical care environments. Built with a powder-coated mild steel body, it features a manual trolley-mounted configuration for easy mobility. The system supports connections for two Oxygen and two Nitrous Oxide cylinders, with additional rear-side mounts for “B” type cylinders. Equipped with a 9" rotameter, oxygen flush, and oxy failure warning device, it ensures safe and precise gas delivery. Its compact design, portability, and optional ventilator integration make it a reliable choice for modern surgical setups.

UHCP COMFORT

UHCP Comfort Anesthesia Machine is a versatile and cost-effective solution designed for operation theatres and critical care units. Constructed with a powder-coated mild steel trolley and stainless steel tabletop tray, it offers durability and ease of mobility. The system includes a 9" rotameter, audio-visual oxygen failure warning device, and an anti-hypoxic pneumatic mechanism for enhanced patient safety. With multiple gas inlet ports for Oxygen and Nitrous Oxide, a 1:3 ratio control, and a changeover mechanism with oxygen flush, UHCP Comfort ensures precise gas delivery and reliable performance in demanding surgical environments.
