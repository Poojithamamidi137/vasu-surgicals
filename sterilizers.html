PSI HORIZONTAL HIGH PRESSURE STEAM STERILIZER

PSI HORIZONTAL HIGH PRESSURE STEAM STERILIZER are robust and high-performance sterilization units designed for hospital and laboratory use. Constructed from SS 304 including the supporting stand, with an optional 316L stainless steel chamber for enhanced corrosion resistance. These units feature a double door configuration for barrier applications and offer a Class B option equipped with a vacuum pump for pre- and post-vacuum cycles. Semi-automatic operation ensures ease of use, while ISI certification by the Bureau of Indian Standards confirms safety and compliance for 121°C versions. Available models include PSI Sterilis 5090 (500 × 900 mm, 180 L), 50120 (500 × 1200 mm, 235 L), and 60120 (600 × 1200 mm, 340 L) — each operating at 18 kW electrical power with 3-phase, 40A input. With heating via electrical mode, a working pressure range of 20/30 PSI, and working temperature from 121°C to 134°C, these sterilizers are tested hydraulically at 60 PSI for maximum safety and reliability

PSI RECTRANGLAR STEAM STERILIZR

PSI Sterilis R Horizontal High Pressure Rectangular Steam Sterilizer is a robust and ISI-marked sterilization system designed for hospital and laboratory use. Manufactured in accordance with IS 3829 Part-1 standards (CM/L-640005090), it features a durable SS 304 construction including the stand, with an optional 316L stainless steel chamber for enhanced corrosion resistance. Available in single-door (SD) and double-door (DD) configurations, the system supports Class B sterilization with a vacuum pump and offers semi-automatic operation for ease of use. Operating at 121°C to 134°C with a working pressure of 20/30 PSI and hydraulic test pressure of 60 PSI, it ensures reliable and efficient sterilization. Both models—PSI Sterilis 6060120 SD and DD—feature a spacious 600 × 600 × 1200 mm chamber with a volume of 435 liters, powered by an 18 kW electrical system requiring 40A on a 3-phase supply.

HIGH PRESSURE VERTICAL STERILIZER

The PSI Sterilis 4060 V Vertical Steam Sterilizer is a compact and efficient solution for sterilizing medical instruments in clinics, laboratories, and small hospital setups. Featuring a double-wall construction for enhanced thermal insulation, it includes pressure overload protection and a pressure switch to maintain stabilized internal conditions. Independent pressure and temperature monitoring ensures precise control during sterilization cycles. The chamber is built from durable SS 304 stainless steel with a 76-liter capacity and dimensions of 400 × 600 mm. Operating at 3 kW electrical power on a single-phase 20A supply, it delivers consistent performance at 121°C and 20 PSI working pressure, with a hydraulic test pressure of 40 PSI for safety assurance.
