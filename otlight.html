SPECTRA LUMIO LED OT.LIGHT

he SPECTRA LUMIO LED OT Light features a Twin Dome setup that delivers powerful illumination up to 160,000 Lux in boost mode. Designed with 72 advanced LED clusters and a unique bowl reflector system, it ensures pure, uniform white light with minimal heat buildup. Its streamlined, lightweight domes support laminar flow, while the autoclavable focusing handle and onboard control panel offer precise adjustments, including Endo ambient lighting at the touch of a button. A high CRI of 96 and adjustable color temperature between 3300K–4800K guarantee excellent visibility during procedures. Built for durability, each dome consumes only 140W and offers a service life of over 50,000 hours

MATRIX -2 LED OT.LIGHT

The MATRIX LED Operation Theatre Light ‘Twin Dome’ Ceiling Model is designed to provide exceptional illumination for surgical procedures. With each dome emitting 140,000 Lux, the lights feature 96 OSRAM LEDs and French Gaggione lenses for a uniform, cool-toned output. The streamlined, ultra-thin heads offer adjustable focus and depth of illumination, supported by spring arms for flexible positioning. Surgeons can fine-tune the color balance and brightness (0%–100%) using the digital control system or an infrared remote. Built with longevity in mind, each dome consumes only 50W and is engineered for up to 50,000 hours of continuous use.

RSM LED OT.LIGHT

RSM LED OT.LIGHT are specialized lighting systems used in operating theaters (OT) to provide high-intensity, shadow-free, and adjustable lighting for surgical procedures. These lights use LED (Light Emitting Diode) technology, offering numerous advantages over traditional lighting systems, such as energy efficiency, long lifespan, and reduced heat emission. Surgical LED OT lights are designed to meet the strict requirements of the medical environment, ensuring that the surgical team has the optimal lighting conditions for performing precise and delicate surgeries.
