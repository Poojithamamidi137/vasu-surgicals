SUCTION APPARATUS

Suction apparatus systems are essential devices used in hospitals and surgical settings for the removal of secretions, blood, or other fluids from a patient's airway or surgical site. Designed for reliability and ease of operation, these units typically include powerful vacuum pumps, collection jars with overflow protection, and adjustable pressure controls to meet various clinical needs. Their portability, hygienic build, and compatibility with standard medical accessories make them indispensable in ICUs, operating theatres, and emergency rooms.
